Privacy Policy

We follow local-first storage and data minimization principles to help you manage your private photos and videos.

Last updated: May 15, 2026

Welcome to VideoSafe (the "App"). We care deeply about your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use the App.

Important: VideoSafe's core privacy features are performed on your device. Your private media is stored by default in app-private or encrypted local storage and is not uploaded to our servers.

1. Information We Collect and Use

1.1 Media Files

To provide private album, import, viewing, export, and management features, the App needs access to photos, videos, or files that you choose. Those media files are used only to complete the actions you request and are stored locally on your device or in a backup location that you select.

1.2 Password and Biometric Verification

The App can protect access with a password, Face ID, or Touch ID. Your original password is not uploaded to our servers. Face ID and Touch ID are handled by Apple's system APIs. We do not collect, store, or transmit your raw biometric data; we only receive the verification result.

1.3 Camera and Intrusion Capture

If you use capture or intrusion capture features, the App may access the camera. Intrusion capture photos are created only after you enable the feature and are stored locally on your device to help you review suspicious unlock attempts.

1.4 Device and Diagnostic Information

To improve compatibility, troubleshoot issues, and improve the user experience, we may process non-directly identifying device or diagnostic information, such as device model, operating system version, app version, crash logs, and basic runtime status.

2. Permissions

The App may request the following permissions when you use related features:

Photos and media access

Used to import, view, export, and manage photos and videos that you choose.

Files access

Used to import media files from the Files app or export files.

Camera permission

Used for taking photos or videos and for intrusion capture after you enable it.

Local network permission

Used for Wi-Fi transfer or to discover casting devices on the same local network.

You can manage these permissions in system settings. Disabling a permission may make the related feature unavailable, but other basic features may continue to work.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Private media is stored by default in app-private or encrypted storage that other apps cannot directly access.
  • Password-related information is stored securely. We cannot recover your original password.
  • Uninstalling the App, clearing app data, device damage, or forgetting your password may make data unrecoverable.
  • We recommend regularly exporting or backing up important content, especially before switching devices, reinstalling the system, or uninstalling the App.

4. Cloud Sync and Third-Party Services

If you actively use cloud sync, Google Drive, email feedback, App Store subscriptions, system sharing, casting, or other third-party services, Apple, Google, or your selected service provider may process necessary data. Third-party services are governed by their own privacy policies and terms.

Except for content you actively choose to upload, back up, export, or share, we do not upload your private media to our servers.

5. Subscriptions and Purchase Information

If you purchase an auto-renewable subscription, purchase, renewal, refund, and billing information is processed by Apple App Store. We may receive non-sensitive membership status information from Apple or a subscription service provider, such as whether a subscription is active, expiration date, and product identifier, to unlock premium features.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We process or share necessary information only in the following situations:

  • With your explicit authorization or when you initiate export, backup, sharing, feedback, or similar actions;
  • To provide app features through services such as Apple or Google;
  • To comply with applicable laws, court orders, or lawful government requests;
  • To protect the rights, safety, and legitimate interests of users, the developer, or the public.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information without guardian consent, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

8. Your Choices and Rights

  • You can delete, export, or manage local media content inside the App.
  • You can revoke permissions such as Photos, Camera, and Local Network access in system settings.
  • You can uninstall the App to stop using the service, but uninstalling may delete local data.
  • You can contact us using the email below for privacy-related support.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect product changes, legal requirements, or review requirements. The updated policy will be made available in the App, on our website, or on the App Store page. Continued use of the App means you accept the updated policy.
